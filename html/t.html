My Good Thought

Let us kill the Raavana of ego and anger within us this Dussera and make the world a better place.

Best wishes for the day.

Where can we find the courage to act in spite of fear? Trying to eliminate that which we react to fearfully is a fool's errand because it locates the source of our fear outside ourselves, rather than within our own hearts. Leadership in organizing is rooted in three questions articulated by the first-century Jerusalem sage, Rabbi Hillel: "If I am not for myself, who am I? When I am only for myself, what am I? And if not now, when?"
